How do I edit my account credentials?
To edit your account information, first return to home. There you will find three main tabs. Scroll toward the second tab labeled 'Other Stuff' and click on the link labeled 'Account'. In Account, you will find two other links labled 'Edit Account' and 'Delete Account'. Click Edit Account. In Edit Account, you will then find 'Edit Name' and 'Edit Username'. Follow the instructions within each to complete the processes.
What does ID mean?
When patients are registered, they are each given a special number called an ID. This is so their information does not get collided with another patient who may own the same name, age, or birth date. To find the ID, either scroll down to 'All Patients' or search for the patient in the search bar. You will find a hashtag followed by a number above the rest of the information. This is that patient's ID.

In the meantime, it would be advised to create a new account. We apologize for this inconvience.
